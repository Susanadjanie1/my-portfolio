About Me

I am a passionate web developer with a curious mind, a love for learning, and a knack for solving problems. My journey into tech was not linear—it began in the world of economics and financial services, where I developed a sharp analytical mindset and a detail-driven approach to work. I have always been fascinated by systems—how they work, how they break, and most importantly, how they can be improved.

During my time in auditing and document coordination, I worked with numbers, processes, and people. I became someone who could quickly spot inefficiencies and offer practical, structured solutions. But something was missing: creativity and innovation. That is when I discovered front-end development—and everything clicked.

Since then, I have immersed myself in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js, building responsive and accessible web applications that not only look good but also perform exceptionally well. I love the process of turning ideas into real, usable products that can make someone's life easier, more efficient, or more delightful.

As a junior front-end developer, I am focused on growing my skills every single day. Whether it is through building personal projects, contributing to open source, or learning from seasoned developers, I am all in.

But I am not just here to code—I am here to build a brand. As a visionary entrepreneur at heart, I aspire to create meaningful digital products, contribute to the tech community, and one day lead my own tech-driven business that makes a real impact, especially in Africa.

When I am not coding, you will probably find me exploring business ideas, researching economic trends, or dreaming up the next big project that brings together technology and purpose.
